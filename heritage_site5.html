Site 5:SATABDI PARK

Satabdi Park, located in the heart of Asansol, West Bengal, is a serene urban retreat maintained by Indian Railways(Eastern). Spanning approximately 20 acres, the park offers lush green lawns, vibrant flower beds, and shaded walkways, providing a peaceful environment for relaxation and leisure activities. It features a children's play area equipped with swings, slides, and other play equipment, making it an ideal spot for families. The park also houses a musical fountain and several amusement rides, including a merry-go-round and boating facilities, enhancing its appeal to visitors of all ages. Statues and sculptures, such as one of Rabindranath Tagore, add cultural significance to the park. Easily accessible from Asansol Railway Station, Satabdi Park serves as a popular destination for picnics, nature walks, and community gatherings.

Site 8: BURNPUR RIVERSIDE

The Burnpur Riverside area in Asansol, West Bengal, lies along the banks of the Damodar River and is renowned for its scenic beauty and recreational spaces. A prominent attraction here is Nehru Park, also known as Lahmeyer Park or Riverside Park, established by F.W.A. Lahmeyer of the IISCO Steel Plant. Spanning 64 acres, the park features lush gardens, a musical fountain, boating facilities, and a children's play area, making it a popular spot for families and picnickers. The tranquil ambiance, coupled with views of the Damodar River, offers visitors a peaceful retreat from urban life. The riverside area not only provides leisure opportunities but also reflects the industrial heritage of Burnpur, closely tied to the steel industry. Easily accessible from various parts of Asansol, the Burnpur Riverside area serves as a cherished destination for both locals and tourists seeking relaxation amidst nature.It is mainly famous for it's cbse undertaking school name B.R.S(Burnpur Riverside School).

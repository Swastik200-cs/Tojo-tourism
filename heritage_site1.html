Site 1: Susantas Wax Museum

Wax museum, also known as a waxworks, is a museum that displays lifelike wax sculptures of famous people and historical figures. These sculptures, often displayed in realistic poses and wearing real clothes, are created using intricate techniques, including taking numerous measurements of the model and pouring wax into a mold.A wax museum is a specialized attraction that displays lifelike wax sculptures of famous people, historical figures, celebrities, cultural icons, and fictional characters. These figures are carefully crafted from wax and other materials to closely resemble real individuals, often posed in realistic settings or scenes. The purpose of a wax museum is both educational and entertaining, allowing visitors to learn about and visually engage with significant personalities from history, entertainment, sports, politics, and more. Some museums focus on local heroes or regional history, while others feature global icons. The most famous wax museums, such as Madame Tussauds, are known for their detailed artistry and constantly updated exhibits.

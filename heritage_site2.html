Site 2: MAA KALYANESHWARI TEMPLE

Maa Kalyaneshwari Temple is a revered Hindu temple located near Asansol in West Bengal, India, close to the Maithon Dam on the banks of the Barakar River. Dedicated to Goddess Kalyaneshwari, a form of the Divine Mother, it is believed to be over 500 years old. The temple is a popular pilgrimage site, especially for devotees seeking blessings for children and family welfare. Rich in spiritual significance, it is associated with Shakti worship and attracts visitors from neighboring states like Jharkhand and Bihar.

Site 14:ASANSOL POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE.

Established in 1950, Asansol Polytechnic is a premier government technical institute located in Dhadka, Asansol, West Bengal. Affiliated with the West Bengal State Council of Technical and Vocational Education and Skill Development and approved by AICTE, it offers three-year diploma programs in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Mining, Metallurgical Engineering, and Mine Surveying.The campus spans approximately 68 acres and is equipped with modern laboratories, computer centers, and workshops. Admissions are primarily through the JEXPO entrance exam, requiring candidates to have passed the 10th standard with Science and Mathematics.The institute boasts a strong placement record, with top recruiters including JSPL, L&T, and Bajaj. Committed to producing skilled technical professionals, Asansol Polytechnic remains a significant contributor to technical education in the region.It ranks among top 8 asian polytechnic colleges.

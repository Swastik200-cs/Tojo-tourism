Site 12:CHURULIA.

Churulia is a culturally significant village located about 17 km from Asansol in West Bengal. It is best known as the birthplace of the legendary Bengali poet Kazi Nazrul Islam, often referred to as the "Rebel Poet" and the national poet of Bangladesh. The village houses the Nazrul Academy and a museum that preserves his manuscripts, personal belongings, and photographs. Churulia attracts visitors and literary enthusiasts who come to pay homage and explore his legacy. The annual Nazrul Jayanti celebration draws large crowds, celebrating his contributions to literature, music, and social reform. The serene rural setting, historical relevance, and cultural ambiance make Churulia an inspiring destination.
